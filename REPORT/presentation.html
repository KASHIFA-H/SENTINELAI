PROBLEM STATEMENT
🏭 Industrial OT environments face a ransomware crisis
Manufacturing plants, energy systems, hospitals, and critical infrastructure run on Operational Technology (OT) — PLCs, SCADA servers, HMI terminals. A ransomware attack on these systems doesn't just lose data — it stops physical operations, costing thousands per minute and threatening human safety.
The Gap in Existing Solutions
  • Enterprise tools (Dragos, Claroty) cost $100,000+/year — inaccessible to most organisations
  • General IT tools (CrowdStrike) are not designed for OT environments
  • Backup tools (Veeam) provide recovery but no real-time detection
  • Average ransomware dwell time in OT: 42 days before detection
  • Manual recovery takes 4+ hours — production stops completely
Real-World Impact
  • 2021 Colonial Pipeline — fuel supply to US East Coast shut down
  • 2017 NotPetya — Maersk lost $300 million in one attack
  • OT ransomware incidents up 50% year-on-year (Dragos 2025)
  • Average ransom demand: $4.5 million per incident
  • Manufacturing is the #1 targeted sector globally
